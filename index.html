Situation summary

Morongo Basin families depend on strong, accessible public schools. MUSD is discussing possible closure of up to four elementary schools — Morongo Valley, Landers, Friendly Hills, and Palm Vista — as early as Fall 2027. These closures would affect the whole district, including receiving schools facing larger classes, boundary changes, transportation impacts, staffing shifts, and program changes. Before any closure moves forward, the public deserves real savings numbers, transition costs, alternatives, administrative reduction options, and a clear fiscal stabilization plan. School closures are not just budget decisions. They reshape communities for decades.
